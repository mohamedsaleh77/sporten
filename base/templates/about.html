ABOUT US

Mission & Vision

Mission: Our mission is to empower the UNITEN community to easily access and utilize the university's sports facilities, promoting an active lifestyle for all students and staff.

Vision: We envision a campus where booking sports facilities is effortless, transparent, and efficient, leading to increased participation in sports and recreational activities.

Project Overview

SPORTEN is a web-based booking system designed to achieve this mission and vision. It provides a centralized platform for students and staff to:

Browse: Explore our catalog of available sports facilities.

Book: Effortlessly reserve facilities for specific dates and times, with real-time availability updates and instant confirmation.

Manage: View, modify, or cancel upcoming bookings through a personalized dashboard, simplifying schedule management.
